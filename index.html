The Super Mario Bros. Movie

Italian-American brothers Mario and Luigi have recently opened a plumbing business in Brooklyn, to the derision of their ex-foreman Spike and the disapproval of their father. After seeing a significant water main leak on the news, Mario and Luigi go underground to fix it but are suckedinto a Warp Pipe and separated.

Main Characters:


Mario (Chris Pratt)

Luigi (Charlie Day)

Peach (Anna Taylor-Joy)

Bowser (Jack Black)
